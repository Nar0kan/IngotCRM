Frequently asked questions | FAQ

How do I create my own organization?

By creating new account you will automatically be loged in as an organization. That is why to create agents in your account and let them log in you should consider creating your organization first using registration. After that go to 'Agents' page and 'Add a new agent'. After completing the form new account will be created with the username you entered. A mail will be sent to the mail you have entered with all sing up information. Your agents can sign up at any time by using this data.

What kind of documents can be uploaded?

All organizations can only upload data files in pdf, docx, pptx, xlsx extensions, as only MS Office and PDF formats are supported.

How does support work?

We're aware of the importance of well qualified support, that is why we decided that support will only be provided by the authors that actually worked on this project.

Feel free to contact us and we'll help you out as soon as we can.

I want to build more than one organization. Is that allowed?

You're free to use many accounts as you like. However, don't overpush this posibility, cause it may cause trouble for everyone. We are trying to be nice here :)

Find out more information by reading the license.

What does "Free version" include?

The free version is based on basic needs for organization to see the adventages of IngotCRM. By using free bundle you'l have access to the leads, agents, categories, documents layouts. But uploading document any other format than pdf will be restricted. Also the amount of leads can be up to 50 and agents you can operate with is up to 10.

What does the "Gold version" include?

The Gold version includes a Calendar and a Notes pages for Agents, Comments for Documents, all MS Office formats for documents, like .pptx, .xlsx, .docx, .pdf. Besides, the organization with the "Gold version" can handle up to 500 leads and 100 agents. Additionally, the customer support for "Gold vresion" users is immidiate.

What is the difference between the 'Gold' and the 'Silver' versions?

The 'Silver' version has almost the same features, except the limit for the leads is 200 and for agents 50. Also the Comments for Documents are not involved here. But the support is fast responding too.

What if I find a bug?

If you have discovered any bug, or other discomfortable type of things on the site - feel free to contact us !
